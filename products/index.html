Technical Feasibility Scan

A solid technical fundament is crucial for sustainable success of Internet Startups. To mitigate technical implementation risk StarterSquad can perform a Technical Feasibility Scan. We will:

  • draw up a potential architecture;
  • estimate the amount of work [1];
  • identify technical risks such as: scalability, complexity or need for rare skills.

When investing in a new idea, there are business risks and technical risks. Financial investors ask founders to scrutinize the business risks, but most technical investors [2] are less experienced and fooled by the founders' enthusiasm. An investor should not rely on the founders’ technical team to identify risks early.

Business Viability Scan

StarterSquad has developed a methodology for scanning the viability of your startup or innovation idea. In our evidence-based approach we check your startup against 7 dimensions of successful Startups:

  • Idea: the idea is superb and will make headlines instantly.
  • Team: your team is awesome and will make this idea happen.
  • Product: your product just rocks your customers’ socks off!
  • Market: there is a big and identifiable market for your idea.
  • Execution: the power to realize your dream both technically and commercially.
  • Traction: the proof that your value proposition is appealing to customers.
  • Social Proof: the proof that investors are interested and comparable startups are in business.

The Business Viability Scan consists of a questionnaire, desk research and 2 workshops with your Startup team. It results in a report that can be used for growing your business and acquiring investment. This scan is often combined with the Technical Feasibility Scan when it comes technology-based Startups. Together they provide a roadmap for Lean Startup of your Business.
